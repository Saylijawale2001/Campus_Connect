Christu Jyothi Institute of Technology and Science (CJITS) is a prestigious engineering college located in Yeshwanthapur, Jangoan, Telangana, India. Established in 1998, CJITS has emerged as a leading institution for technical education in the region, known for its commitment to excellence in academics, research, and innovation.

The sprawling campus of CJITS is spread over acres of lush greenery, providing an ideal environment for learning and personal development. The institute boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, modern classrooms, and cutting-edge facilities that foster a conducive learning environment for students pursuing Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) programs.
